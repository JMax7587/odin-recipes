Pepperoni Pizza Casserole

DESCRIPTION

This recipe for cheesy pepperoni pizza casserole includes all of your favorite pizza toppings.

INGREDIENTS:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (8 ounce) package uncooked egg noddles
  • 20 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce, or as needed
  • 1 (8 ounce) package sliced pepperoni, coarsely chopped
  • 1 (2.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1 (2.5 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained

STEPS:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Cook ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until no longer pink, breaking into crumbles as it cooks, about 10 minutes.
  3. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rapid boil. Cook egg noodles at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 5 minutes; drain.
  4. Combine beef, noodles, 1/2 mozzarella cheese, spaghetti sauce, pepperoni, mushrooms, and black olives in a large bowl; transfer to a 9x13-inch baking dish. Top with remaining 1/2 mozzarella cheese. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until casserole is bubbling, 30 to 45 minutes.
